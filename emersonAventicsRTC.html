Rodless cylinders, RTC series
Rodless cylinders, RTC series - advantages at a glance
  • The oval piston enables higher loads and torques, and highly compact designs
  • Minimal leakage and a wide range of speeds thanks to a special internal sealing strip and grease
  • One-piece slide and piston increase stability
The product range: one basis, three variants
The rodless cylinders offer optimized stroke length in a compact size. This saves space and facilitates machine design. The range of applications extends from piston diameters of 16 mm up to 80 mm and stroke lengths up to 9,900 mm. The cylinders feature extreme repeatability and cover a large speed range from 0.01 m/s to >20 m/s.
The comprehensive range with many variants and options enables configurations that are individually tailored to the application.
Basic Version: RTC-BV
Internal guiding thanks to a unique oval piston design. Typical application areas include: door openers for tooling machine, cutting and drive processes.
AVENTICS Cylinder RTC-SB
Slide Bearing: RTC-SB Adjustable slide bearing. Typical application areas: material flow, fibre glass winding.
Compact Guide: RTC-CG
Compact precision guiding. Typical application areas: medium loads in settings with limited space.
Heavy Duty: RTC-HD
High-precision guiding for heavy loads. Typical application areas: as a base axis for handling applications, very high load capacity.
