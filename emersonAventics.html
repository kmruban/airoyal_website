Emerson Aventics
Emerson's innovative pneumatic solutions from AVENTICS is one of the worlds leading manufacturers of pneumatic components and systems for different industries. AVENTICS provide precise and long-lasting operation for your machine automation applications.
Pneumatic Solutions to Solve Your Machine Automation Needs
When OEMs and manufacturers need reliable and innovative pneumatics solutions, they look to Emerson’s extensive AVENTICS portfolio of cylinders, valves, air preparation and pressure regulators, engineered in Germany. With a compact design and low-maintenance technology to actuate machinery, our pneumatics can be trusted to deliver reliable and efficient operation even in the most demanding applications and environments.
Pneumatic Directional Control Valves, Ceram™
Rugged air valves for harsh environments
Engineered to operate in harsh environments where other valves fail, Emerson’s AVENTICS™ Ceram™ pneumatic ISO valves also tolerate dirty or contaminated air supplies. Ceramic plates deliver a near-perfect seal and an extended life of up to 150 million cycles. Choose from many options, including solenoid or air pilot operation and two- or three-position operation.
Rodless cylinders, RTC series
Compact, high-performance RTC rodless cylinders
Put the power you need in the space you have with high-performance Emerson’s AVENTICS™ RTC rodless pneumatic cylinders. This latest generation design offers space-saving versatility, easy installation and adjustable pneumatic end cushioning. Its oval piston allows you to use it in applications requiring higher loads and torques. And, with so many options available, it’s simple to tailor configurations to your application. Available in four versions with seven bore sizes.
PowerMaster® NFPA
(Air & Low Pressure Hydraulic)
Steel NFPA cylinder quick-delivery program
Count on Emerson’s AVENTICS™ NFPA pneumatic and hydraulic cylinders—readily available on our 3-day delivery program for Preferred Standards options. Choose from a full range of standard precision-machined mountings and custom modifications. Design your cylinder using our simple online configuration tool
Clean Line Air Valves, Series CL03
Wash down air valves that handle pressure
Emerson’s AVENTICS™ Clean Line CL03 pneumatic valves are ideal for food processing and packaging machines. Hygienic and corrosion resistant, they withstand IP69K high-pressure wash down. No protective cabinet required. Gaps between the valve protection covers allow for easy cleaning and help prevent dirt pockets. Available in standard manifold, higher flow and a single subbase version. Design your custom valve manifolds online.
Valve System, Series ES05
Simple solutions, efficient pneumatics
Emerson’s AVENTICS™ ES05 pneumatic valve system is the essential solution for standard pneumatic applications. Quickly configure the valve you need with our modular design and online tools. Choose factory assembly or build your own with simple components that make incorrect installation virtually impossible.
ISO 15552 Pneumatic Cylinders, Series PRA/TRB
Pneumatic cylinders, perfectly cushioned
The next generation of ISO 15552 cylinders, Emerson’s AVENTICS™ PRA/TRB series delivers optimized cushioning for smooth-running operation and shorter cycle times while reducing noise and vibration. These cylinders are lightweight and efficient, making them ideal for many tasks and operations across nearly all industries. Choose from numerous options, including linear guides and high-temperature versions.
